always love to draw from drawing figures to still life.
There are some subjects i have yet to draw and learn
from, i have yet to find my artsyle and make my art

Reading - can you even consider this my hobby if i've
only read a couple of books? the genres i like are: fiction,
mystery, fantasy, satire, legend, and drama - to name a few.

about the ill-fated lives of the Baudelaire children: this
series has death, comedy, dark humor, absurd situations; these
are the factors that makes this book a favorite of mine.
